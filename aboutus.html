About GHOR

God's House of Refuge (GHOR) Ministry is a vibrant community dedicated to sharing the love, hope, and power of Jesus Christ. Our mission is to reach out, restore, and empower lives through the Word of God, worship, and service.

About GHOR

A Family of Worship and Impact

God's House of Refuge is a family of passionate worshipers of God fueled by the hunger to change their world. With the paramount character of love, fidelity, accountability, and excellence, this family is committed to meeting the needs of their community through impactful programs which have been their identity over the years. Operating in a conducive atmosphere for spiritual growth and development, this family is structured to see to the individual maturity of the budding believer.

Our Vision

To raise change agents out of you.

Our Mission

To reach, restore and reposition men towards destiny actualization.

Our Core Values

  • Holiness: Righteous living and a transparent lifestyle of integrity.
  • Excellence: Be extremely good at what you do; having high quality and standards.
  • Accountability: Faithful in what God has committed into your hands, being responsible to somebody for something.
  • Love: Practical loyalty and commitment to fellow brethren and the world.
