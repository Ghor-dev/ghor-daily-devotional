About GHOR

God’s House of Refuge (GHOR) is a vibrant Pentecostal Christian church founded by Pastor Ezekiel Atang. We are a family of believers—small enough to know you personally, yet large enough to embrace you fully. Located in Akwa Ibom State, Nigeria, GHOR is a place where the presence and power of God bring salvation, healing, restoration of destinies, and the uplifting of human dignity.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of light and refuge, transforming lives and communities for Christ.

Our Mission

To preach the gospel, nurture spiritual growth, and serve with compassion and excellence.
